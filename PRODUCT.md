# Product

## Register

product

## Users

### Providers
Service professionals who list offerings, manage availability, and handle bookings. Primary screens: Service Management, Booking Dashboard, Earnings/Wallet. They care about visibility, conversion, and operational efficiency.

### Customers
End users who discover services, book appointments, and leave reviews. Primary screens: Home/Discovery, Service Detail, Booking Flow, My Bookings, Profile. They care about finding the right service quickly, seamless booking, and loyalty rewards.

### Admins
Platform operators who oversee marketplace health. Primary screens: Dashboard, User Management, Service Approval, Analytics. They care about service quality, provider performance, and platform metrics.

## Product Purpose

A home services marketplace where discovery, booking, and loyalty converge. Providers monetize expertise; customers get quality-assured services with a points-based rewards system that drives retention. The platform's success is measured by completed bookings, repeat customers, and provider satisfaction.

## Brand Personality

**Loyalty-first, effortlessly premium.** The experience feels curated and trustworthy — not transactional. Points and rewards are woven into every interaction, making repeat engagement feel natural rather than forced. Warm, calm, and confident — like a well-run boutique rather than a discount platform.

## Anti-references

- **No dark themes.** Light, airy surfaces only. Warm cream/blush palette.
- **No aggressive gamification.** Points feel like earned value, not arcade tokens.
- **No SaaS-generic aesthetics.** Skip the navy-and-white enterprise look, the "modern dashboard" grid, and the gradient hero syndrome.
- **No rushed booking flows.** Every step should feel deliberate, not a funnel to maximize conversions.

## Design Principles

1. **Loyalty is the throughline.** Points, tiers, and rewards appear consistently — not just at checkout. Every action that benefits the platform should feel recognized.

2. **Warmth over cold efficiency.** The aesthetic is luxury-minimal with NILIN's signature blush/cream palette. Interactions feel human, not automated.

3. **Discovery is effortless.** Customers should find what they need in under 3 taps. Search, filters, and AI recommendations work together.

4. **Providers are partners.** Dashboard surfaces are clear, actionable, and respect their time. No information overload.

5. **Trust is visible.** Service quality signals (ratings, reviews, verification badges) are prominent. Booking feels safe.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance target
- Focus on color contrast — warm grays must meet 4.5:1 against cream backgrounds
- Reduced motion support throughout (prefers-reduced-motion respected)
- Keyboard navigable modals, dropdowns, and booking flows
- Screen reader friendly form labels and error states
